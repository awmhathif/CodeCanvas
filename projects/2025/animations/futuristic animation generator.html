Animation Controls

Click the buttons below to add different animation elements:

  • Orbs: Floating glowing spheres
  • Lines: Geometric connecting lines
  • Hexagons: Floating hexagonal shapes
  • Cubes: 3D rotating cubes
  • Vortex: Spinning vortex effect
  • Pulse: Expanding ring pulses
  • Reset: Clear all animations

Click anywhere on the screen to create effects at that position.

Use the color buttons to change the theme.
