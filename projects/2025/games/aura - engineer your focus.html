ADVANCED FOCUS PROTOCOLS

Select a training module. These advanced protocols are designed to fundamentally upgrade your brain's hardware for focus.

Real-Time Neurofeedback

Simulated EEG data provides instant feedback on your brainwave activity. Learn to consciously guide your mind into states of high focus (Beta waves) and calm alertness (Alpha waves).

Binaural Beat Entrainment

Listen to specifically engineered audio frequencies that encourage your brain to synchronize with patterns associated with deep concentration and problem-solving.

Attention Filtering

Engage in exercises that train you to consciously filter out irrelevant auditory and visual stimuli, strengthening your brain's natural "spam filter" and improving signal-to-noise ratio.

Sensory Deprivation Simulation

Guided practices that teach you to find focus in silence and darkness. By temporarily reducing sensory input, you recalibrate and heighten your attentional control when you return to a normal environment.

Working Memory Drills

Perform a series of adaptive 'n-back' tasks and other brain games designed to systematically expand your working memory capacity, allowing you to hold and manipulate more information at once.

Dual-Task Interference

Practice performing two cognitive tasks simultaneously. This advanced training forces your prefrontal cortex to become more efficient at resource allocation and task management under pressure.

The Challenge/Skill Ratio

Learn to identify and operate at the precise intersection where a task is difficult enough to be engaging but not so hard it causes anxiety. This is the gateway to flow.

Immediate Feedback Loops

Structure your work and practice to provide constant, real-time feedback on your performance. This clarity of progress is a key trigger for entering and maintaining a flow state.
