How to Play

Place turrets along the winding road to stop enemies from reaching the exit portal.

  • Standard Turret: Medium fire rate, average range/damage.
  • Rapid-Fire Turret: High fire rate, short range, lower damage.
  • Heavy Turret: Low fire rate, long range, high damage with splash effect.

Click near the road to place a turret of the currently selected type.

Enemies spawn from the start portal and follow the road. Prevent them from reaching the exit!

Score increases for each enemy defeated. The stage counter increases after each wave.

You can upgrade turrets and adjust settings in the panel at the bottom-right.
