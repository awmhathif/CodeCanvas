🔬 About This Simulation

This is a real-time simulation of a black hole accretion disk, showing matter spiraling into the event horizon. The visualization includes gravitational lensing effects, temperature-based coloring (blue = hottest, red = coolest), relativistic jets, and Doppler beaming effects. Matter heats up as it approaches the event horizon, emitting intense radiation before crossing the point of no return.
