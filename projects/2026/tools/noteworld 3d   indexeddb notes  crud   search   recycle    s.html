3D controls: drag to orbit · scroll to zoom · click a planet to select · double‑click to open. Keys: N new, / focus search, F focus planet, Del to recycle/restore, P pin, Esc close editor.
Creative bits: notes become planets; tags affect orbit ring color; pinned notes glow; deleted notes drift into a “graveyard” orbit. Search reshapes the whole system.
